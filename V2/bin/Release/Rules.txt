Each player may create one set of ten random items.
Each player may switch out any or all of there items for ones in the pre genarated sets.
Each player also starts out with 300 gold in coins or currency.
After two hours of play a player may create a new set and exchange any two items between the old and new sets
then may switch out any or all items with ones in the pre genarated sets (the old set is then discarded).
